Yujia Huang

California Institute of Technology
yjhuang@caltech.edu

I am a PhD student at Caltech supervised by Prof. Anima Anandkumar. My research interests lie on the intersection of deep learning and neuroscience. I hope to design “less artificial” artificial intelligence. I am currently working on neuro-inspired generative models and probabilistic inference towards the goal. Previously, I received my bachelor degree in Electrical Engineering from Zhejiang University in 2017 with a focus on optics. I have worked in Biophotonics Lab on imaging inside scattering media before joining Prof. Anandkumar’s lab.
